# Resource Summary

## From ICS-201 Form #10

Resource Summary Enter the following information about the resources allocated to the
incident.

- **Resource Identifier:** Enter the relevant agency designator and/or resource designator (if
any).
- **Resource:** Enter the number and appropriate category, kind, or type of resource
ordered.
- **Date/Time Ordered:** Enter the date (month/day/year) and time (24-hour clock) the resource
was ordered.
- **ETA:** Enter the estimated time of arrival (ETA) to the incident (use 24-hour clock).
- **Arrived:** Enter an “X” or a checkmark upon arrival to the incident.
- **Notes (location/assignment/status):** Enter notes such as the assigned location of the resource and/or the actual assignment and status.
